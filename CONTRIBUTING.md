# Contributing

Contributions should keep F-UI focused on source-code learning, technical discussion, lawful private lab use, security, auditability, and operational clarity.

The project will not accept changes, examples, documentation, issue templates, or discussions that are primarily intended to:

- provide, market, or normalize a public proxy, VPN, telecom, hosting, or resale service;
- bypass laws, regulatory controls, platform terms, or third-party access controls;
- hide abuse, weaken auditability, or remove meaningful permission checks;
- automate spam, scanning, credential theft, attacks, infringement, fraud, or other unlawful use.

Do not submit secrets, real deployment files, private account IDs, private domains, private emails, or tokens.

See [DISCLAIMER.md](DISCLAIMER.md) for the controlling risk notice.
